Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ApexFlow's decentralized GPU rental platform, AI-driven automation, and $APEXFLOW token.

What is ApexFlow?

ApexFlow is a decentralized GPU rental marketplace powered by blockchain and AI. It allows developers, researchers, and creators to rent high-performance GPUs instantly through a conversational AI chatbot, eliminating the need for complex interfaces or technical expertise.

How do I rent a GPU with ApexFlow?

Simply chat with ApexFlow bot, our AI assistant. Describe your computational needs, and ApexFlow bot will match you with the best available GPU. You can rent GPUs by the minute or hour, paying only for what you use.

What payment methods does ApexFlow support?

ApexFlow transactions are powered by the $APEXFLOW token. Users can acquire $APEXFLOW through supported exchanges or liquidity pools and use it for GPU rentals.

What GPUs are available on ApexFlow?

We offer high-performance GPUs, including:

  • NVIDIA A100 (Ideal for AI model training, 3D rendering, and quantum simulations)
  • RTX 4090 (Best for Stable Diffusion, real-time rendering, and scientific computing)

Is there a minimum rental time?

No, you can rent GPUs on a flexible pay-as-you-go basis, with charges calculated by the minute.

What is the total supply of $APEXFLOW?

The total supply of $APEXFLOW is 1,000,000,000 tokens, with 0% tax on transactions.

How do I get started with ApexFlow?

Click [Start Chatting with ApexFlow Bot] to begin. Our AI assistant will guide you through the process of renting or contributing GPUs.
